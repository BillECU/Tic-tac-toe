# Tic-tac-toe

It is a simple AI that plays tic-tac-toe. 
You can choose to play first or second hand againest the AI.

The gameboard looks as follows.

1 2 3

4 5 6

7 8 9

If you choose to play first and choose 1, the gameboard will be

O 2 3

4 5 6

7 8 9
